Install nettools via Google Play Store


The Google Play Store now requires that an app must be tested by 20 users before it can apply for release. Currently, nettools is still in the testing phase. According to Google's regulations, you need to follow these steps to install the app in the testing phase:


1. Join the app's newsgroup. This newsgroup is only used as a credential for installing nettools and will not send any spam messages.

1) Visit https://groups.google.com in your browser and sign in with your Google account.

2) Click the dropdown box and change "My groups" to "All groups and messages".

3) In the Search box, type in "nettoolsgroup" and press Enter. You will find the "nettoolsgroup" Group, described as "nettools app group".

4) Click to join, and then follow the prompts.


2. Install the app using one of the following methods:

1) On your Android phone, visit https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.cky.nettools, and then follow the prompts.

2) On your computer, visit https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.cky.nettools, and then follow the prompts.


3. Now, you can start using it.

Since the app needs to be tested by 20 users before it can be officially released, if possible, please use this app every day for 14 consecutive days. This will count as a qualified test. Thank you for your help.
